We love our pets at Blue Ribbon Hardwoods! There's almost always a dog and a cat with me in the shop while I'm working. One of my very first pieces I built from a wine barrel was a simple dog bed. Since then we have significantly improved our designs. The round half barrel dog beds are great for dogs 10-40 lbs, and the long half barrel beds accommodate the larger breeds comfortably.
